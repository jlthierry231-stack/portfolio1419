# The 5 Fold Awakening

A first-person adventure game blending magic and technology, inspired by Star Wars and Harry Potter.

## Storyline

**Title: The Fivefold Awakening**

**Genre:** Sci-Fi Fantasy • Futuristic Tech + Ancient Magic • Hero’s Journey

**Protagonist:** Kael Ardin, age 10
- Born with dormant affinity for all five elements (Earth, Water, Fire, Air, Spirit)
- Trained his whole life but cannot access a single spark of magic
- Lives in a technologically advanced continent where magic is regulated, scanned, and ranked

**Main Companion / Love Interest:** Lyra Vale
- Same age as Kael
- Stunning beauty, wins youth pageants, featured in local holo-magazines
- Fiercely loyal, smart, kind, and the only one who always believed in Kael
- Discovers she also has a rare magical anomaly

### Game Chapters (Adapted from 54-Chapter Book Outline)

**Book I — THE BOY WITH NO MAGIC (Levels 1–3)**

1. **The Child With All Affinities and No Power**
   - Explore your home, interact with tech-mage parents, experience failed magic tests.
   - Lyra comforts you after another failure.

2. **Academy of Sparks**
   - Enter the futuristic magic academy with holo-construct tutors.
   - Navigate classrooms, face mockery from students for being "Zero-Spark."

3. **A Friend Like No Other**
   - Lyra defends you from bullies; explore the academy grounds.
   - Discover hints of Lyra's secret.

4. **Scanners Can’t Explain It**
   - Undergo a full-spectrum elemental scan in the lab.
   - Results show top 0.01% potential but zero output.

5. **Training Drones and Wooden Swords**
   - Excel in non-magical combat simulations.
   - Train with Lyra, who shows subtle jealousy.

6. **The Lesson of Endurance**
   - Push through a grueling simulation, get injured.
   - Lyra helps you to the infirmary.

7. **Rumors of Dark Mages**
   - News spreads via holonet; security tightens.
   - Investigate rumors in the academy.

8. **The Festival of Lights**
   - Attend district celebration; Lyra wins an award.
   - Feel insecure, but she reassures you.

9. **The Night the Vision Came**
   - Collapse during festival; enter spirit realm vision.

10. **The Ancient Archmage Appears**
    - Meet Archmage Elion in the vision.
    - Learn your destiny as the Fivefold Mage to stop Malakar the Voidborn.

**Future Levels:** Continue with Books II-IV, including quests to awaken powers, battles with dark mages, exploration of ancient ruins, and climactic confrontations.

## Features

- **First-Person Perspective**: Immersive 3D exploration using Three.js
- **Magic Elements**: Cast spells, interact with magical crystals
- **Technology Elements**: Droids, futuristic environments
- **Story Integration**: Narrative progression with key events
- **Controls**:
  - WASD: Move
  - Mouse: Look around
  - Space: Jump
  - Click: Cast spell (consumes mana)
  - E: Advance story (when available)
  - E: Advance story (when available)
- **HUD**: Health, Mana, Inventory, Story display

## How to Play

1. Use WASD to move around the scene
2. Move mouse to look around
3. Click to cast a magic bolt (yellow sphere)
4. Press E near story triggers to advance the narrative
5. Explore the crystal and droid for interactions
